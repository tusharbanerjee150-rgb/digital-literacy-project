Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share OTP, passwords, or bank details
- Always check website URLs before entering information
- Use strong and unique passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
- Verify emails and messages before clicking links
- Keep your apps and software updated
- Use trusted apps for online payments
- Regularly monitor bank transactions
- Report cybercrime at cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930
